Caverns & Krakens

Our goal is to use the framework of Dungeons & Dragons to tell a dynamic story for the whole family.

Ekanem Umut

A dragonborn monk who recently mastered the ways of the four elements. Ekanem hates politicians and adheres to utilitarianism.

Mr. Pickles

A barbarian half-orc who was raised by a commune of changelings. Mr. Pickles left the commune after his best friend, Todd the sheep, died.

Momo Walker

An imposing-looking yet friendly polar bugbear. He initially claimed to be a bard but has been revealed to the party to be a warlock who made a deal with a demon for his powers. He has yet to give more details but frequently speaks to his “invisible” best friend, Frank.

Scar

An ex-pirate leonin paladin who worships the goddess Helia.

Squiggles "Jimbo"
the Kraken

This juvenile kraken was flung into the material plane around the time of the Great Flood. He is the last red kraken and unlike most krakens, he is not evil.

The Postdiluvian Realms

Caverns & Krakens takes place in a modified version of Faerun with a homebrew pantheon of gods. 10 years ago, a devastating flood occurred that covered 90% of the world's landmass and drastically altered international politics. This flood has come to be known as The Great Flood or simply The Flood.
